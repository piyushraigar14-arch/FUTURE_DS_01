### Online Retail Sales Analysis



#### Project Overview

This project analyzes an **Online Retail dataset** to uncover key business insights.  

The workflow includes:

\- Cleaning and organizing raw sales data (CSV/Excel).

\- Analyzing revenue trends over time.

\- Identifying top-selling products.

\- Highlighting high-value regions.

\- Building a professional Tableau dashboard.

\- Writing clear recommendations for business decisions.





##### Data Cleaning

Steps performed before analysis:

\- Removed rows with missing product descriptions.

\- Dropped duplicate transactions.

\- Converted `InvoiceDate` to proper datetime format.

\- Filtered out invalid values (negative quantities/prices).

\- Created a new column:

##### Insights

\- Seasonal peaks in sales (Major demand spikes after September).

\- A small set of products drive most revenue.

\- Certain regions contribute disproportionately to overall sales.





##### Recommendations

\- Focus marketing on top-selling products.

\- Expand distribution in high-value regions.

\- Plan inventory around seasonal demand spikes (Majorly during winter season).
